Toriola's Recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water (about 40–45°C)
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (or any cooking oil)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 Cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 Table spoon groung cinnemon
  • 1/2 Cup butter

Instruction

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and oil into the flour gradually.
  4. Mix until a sticky dough begins to form.
  5. Transfer to a floured surface and knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for 1–2 hours until doubled in size.
